Are there trends in how people experience the passing of time from week to week?

Most have had the feeling of a long day, or a short day, and we are quick to write off this perception at the individual level with factors like biochemistry, levels of entertainment, busyness, etc. However, I thought it would be interesting to collect data at the group level to see if there are any trends in individuals' perception of time passing (e.g. in a given week the group strongly votes that it seemed like a long week). So, that's what I'm going to do -- fill out the form with your perception of how quickly or slowly the current week is going.

There's not much of a point to what I'm doing, but I promise I'll waste time (had to) analyzing the data, and will continually update this page as results come in. Join the email list below to be notified when the first results are in, or just check back in a week or two.
